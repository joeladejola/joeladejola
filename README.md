# Joel Adejola

I build decision infrastructure for professional sport and long-horizon human-AI knowledge work.

STATSWING is the applied vertical: AI-native decision infrastructure for football/soccer recruitment, player assessment, and auditable reasoning chains for expensive roster decisions.

Dialectic is the broader research substrate: a working prototype for persistent human-AI collaboration, designed around provenance, critique, memory, unresolved questions, delegation, evaluation, and operator authority.

I study philosophy and physics at the University of Kansas. Before this, I worked as an investigative journalist covering sports and elections, a football scout/strategist, and an Applications Engineering intern at Texas Instruments.
